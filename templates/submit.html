What happens next

We review the submission within about a week. If we need more information (licensing, service area, hours) we'll email the contact person. Once approved, your listing appears on your city page and on any service pages that match.

What this costs

Nothing. There's no paid tier, no "featured" placement that agencies can buy, and no lead-sales tier. The site runs on display ads; agencies don't pay to be here.

How families find you

People Google "home care in [city]" or a specific service, land on the relevant state or city page, and call agencies directly. We don't route calls, collect lead data, or pass your contact info through an intermediary.
