About Senior Home Care Finder

We help families find trusted in-home care for their aging loved ones. Our directory makes it easy to compare agencies, understand your options, and connect with compassionate caregivers.

Our Mission

Finding the right home care shouldn't be overwhelming. When a family member needs assistance, you want answers quickly — not endless phone calls and confusing research. Senior Home Care Finder provides a clear, comprehensive directory of in-home care agencies across the United States.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive listings: Browse thousands of home care agencies across all 50 states
  • Detailed profiles: View services offered, payment options accepted, and contact information
  • Local search: Find agencies in your city or state
  • Service filters: Search by care type — companion care, personal care, dementia care, and more
  • Free to use: Our directory is completely free for families

Our Commitment

We understand that choosing home care is a deeply personal decision. Our goal is to provide accurate, helpful information so you can make the best choice for your family. We don't make decisions for you — we give you the tools to make informed decisions yourself.

The Story Behind This Site

Senior Home Care Finder was created by Kevin Collins, inspired by his family's experience with hospice care for his father — a WWII Navy veteran who battled cancer in 1988 — and again for his mother eleven years later. Those experiences showed Kevin firsthand how the right home care can bring comfort, dignity, and peace to families during their most difficult days.
